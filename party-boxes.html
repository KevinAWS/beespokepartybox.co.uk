What's in a
Party Box?

If you are looking for a children’s/adult’s birthday party, baby shower, or something else, we can create a Beespoke Party Box especially for you.

Prices vary based on theme, number of party guests and contents of the box. A standard Beespoke Party Box for a party of 10 starts from £28.

Just let us know what theme you would like and we can organise the rest. The pictures below show some of the more popular parties, but let us know your chosen theme and we can design a party around that. There are currently 20 party themes in stock, and many of the ranges can also be mixed and matched to suit your requirements and create a unique party.

Each box will be based on the number of party guests, so you can buy exact quantities for your party rather than being confined to buying packs of eight for example. This saves wasting items and also means you are only paying for what you need.

Each Beespoke Party Box contains as standard beautifully coordinated:

Paper Plates

Paper Cups

Paper Straws

Tablecover(s)

Napkins

Balloons
