What's in a
Party Box?

Each bespoke party box contains a choice of plates, cups, straws, tablecover, napkins and balloons based on the number of party guests. You can also add items such as personalised homemade bunting, pass the parcel packages and filled party bags. These items will be lovingly packaged in a box and posted to you in plenty of time for your party; all you need to do is set up on the day, as the planning is all taken care of.

These boxes will be ideal for busy bees who may not have time or find party planning too daunting; Beespoke Party Box offers you a top quality, one-stop bespoke service.
