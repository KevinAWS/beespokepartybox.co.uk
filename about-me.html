Who is behind
Beespoke Party Box

My name is Jenny and I’m mummy to two adorable daughters. Pre children I had worked as a senior audit manager for a large accountancy firm which took me all over the world. I was then blessed to be able to stay at home for 6 years to enjoy bringing up my children.

I love throwing birthday parties for the girls and friends always commented that I should be in the party business; so I thought why not! And here I am! I launched Beespoke Party Box in April 2017 and have not looked back since.

I am passionate about creating amazing parties and can take the pressure of planning a party off your hands. See my facebook page at www.Facebook.com/BeespokePartyBox for lots of lovely party ideas. I’ll think of all the little details to ensure your party is fabulous.

Contact me today to see what your next party could be!
