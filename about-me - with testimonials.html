Who is behind
Beespoke Party Box

My name is Jenny and I’m mummy to two adorable daughters. Pre children I had worked as a senior audit manager for a large accountancy firm which took me all over the world. I was then blessed to be able to take a career break for 5.5 years to enjoy bringing up my children.

I love throwing birthday parties for the girls and friends always commented that I should be in the party business; so I thought why not! And here I am!

I am passionate about creating amazing parties and can take the pressure of planning a party off your hands. I’ll think of all the little details to ensure your party is fabulous; each box also comes with a pack of essential items which you are likely to need for your party – Blu tack, Scissors and Bin bags to help tidy up post party.

Contact me today to see what your next party could bee!

Jenny xx
